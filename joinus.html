Join us

We are looking for entrepreneurial people that love working with others on technology that improves people’s lives. Our main focus are mobile apps but we’re always looking into the future and exploring things like AI, AR, IoT, and more. We like to get the job done but we also like to have fun.
Check out the open positions.

Enough reasons
to say YES

  • Become part of a
    friendly team

    When asked what they love the most about MFA Team, the majority of people working here mentioned the team, the people. It’s an atmosphere where colleagues become friends, some even say “family”.

  • Own shares in our apps

    Many people dream of starting a business and not having to live paycheck to paycheck. Besides building apps for our clients, we’re also launching our own products and everyone in the team who becomes part of a product, gets to own shares. And we’re not talking about 0.3%. Here’s how soon can this happen.

  • Grow and be empowered

    MFA Team is a great place to develop your talents and abilities. You will be assigned a senior member of our team who will help you learn new things and take initiative. Whether it’s coding, design, marketing, or management, you'll have someone who will inspire and guide you.

  • Enjoy a flexible schedule

    You can pick when you start and when you end your work day, but usually between 10 AM and 4 PM we’re available in the office. If something urgent occurs, of course we'll understand.

  • Extend your vacation

    Going on vacation is fun, so for every year you spend with us, we’re adding 1 extra day to your vacation days.

  • Eat all the kebab

    Usually, every Friday we go downtown for a kebab or some other healthy food.

  • Get fit

    We're not 'hard core' sports people, we just like to do things together, like playing soccer-tennis or ping-pong.

  • Get away with us

    At least twice a year we go somewhere remote. We enjoy pools, going hiking, grilled meat on the barbecue, and not to mention “2 cold beers” ®.

  • Support the tech community

    MFA Team is a founding member of Oradea Tech Hub, a local tech community, so you’ll get the chance to get involved.
