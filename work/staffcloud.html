Staffcloud

Staffcloud was a web application, conceived for easy planning of the most complex projects. Projects can take place at several locations, on various days and times, as well with various tasks, assigned to team members. The scope of work was to expand the support and build the native iOS and Android mobile apps. Mobile app users are able to add their providers, in order to download all their assigned events for which they can upload worklogs, custom forms and live timestamps.

Client

Staffcloud is a product developed by SmartBridge, a Swiss company that contacted us by email to discuss a possible cooperation. After a few phone calls and exchanging emails between the general managers and business analyst, Yves - the CEO of SmartBridge was convinced that Mobiversal is the type of company that can build the next version of their app from scratch.
